#  AI Plant Disease Detection App (AgroCare)

##  Project Overview

AI Plant Disease Detection App is an agriculture-based application that uses Artificial Intelligence and Image Processing technology to identify plant diseases from leaf images.

Farmers can upload a plant leaf image, and the AI system analyzes the image to detect diseases, provide disease information, and suggest possible treatment methods. This helps farmers detect diseases early and reduce crop loss.

---

##  Problem Statement

Agriculture faces major challenges due to plant diseases that affect crop production and quality. Many farmers cannot identify diseases at an early stage because of a lack of expert knowledge and limited access to agricultural support.

The AI Plant Disease Detection App solves this problem by using AI technology to analyze plant leaf images and provide quick disease detection with treatment recommendations.

---

##  Objectives

- Detect plant diseases using AI
- Help farmers identify diseases early
- Reduce crop damage and losses
- Provide simple treatment suggestions
- Support smart farming practices
